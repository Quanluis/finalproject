History of the Kappa

What is a Kappa?

A mythological creature that inhabits the Rivers and ponds of Japan. The Kappa possesses the humanoid and reptilian structure of a turtle causing mischief and preying on the unlucky few with trauma or death.

Characteristics

Kappas are usually depicted or drawn with green skin, funny-looking heads and a turtle shell for protection. It's said that the top of their heads is filled with water and most of their power is channeled within it. Some say the best way to defeat one is to hit it on the head.

Pop culture

In pop culture, the Kappa has influenced many creators such as those in video games, movies and art such as anime. A few examples of these creatures can be seen in Pokemon, Nintendos bowser character, the ninja turtles and animes such as Sarazanmai.

Kappa Diet

Kappas usually eat a wide range of foods those including Salamanders, insects, Humans and cucumbers. They're known for walking around with cucumbers in their hands for energy and to wack their victims with.
